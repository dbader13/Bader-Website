Computer Museum of America, 2025 Hall of Fame Inductees

Feb 4, 2025

Meet our 2025 Hall of Fame Inductees! ✨

We’re thrilled to announce that three trailblazing leaders in technology will be honored at BYTE25 on March 6. These pioneers have shaped the tech industry and their legacy continues to inspire.

  • 🏆 David A. Bader, PhD – Revolutionized High-Performance Computing. Nearly all supercomputers being used today, can trace their lineage directly to Dr. Bader’s pioneering work.
  • 🏆 Dan Bricklin – Referred to as the “Father of the Spreadsheet.” He was co-creator of VisiCalc, the first spreadsheet program for personal computers.
  • 🏆 John Yates – A leader in technology law and in shaping Atlanta’s thriving tech ecosystem.

Join us as we celebrate their incredible achievements and raise support for the future of tech history by purchasing a ticket to BYTE25 at CMoA! :party_popper: [Iink: https://lnkd.in/emBt5JsF]
